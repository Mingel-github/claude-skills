Author(s): Parkinson, B. W.; Axelrad, P.
Title: Autonomous GPS Integrity Monitoring Using the Pseudorange Residual
Source: NAVIGATION, 1988, 35(2), pp.255-274
Year: 1988
DOI: 10.1002/j.2161-4296.1988.tb00955.x
Abstract: The use of GPS for navigation-critical applications such as aircraft nonprecision approach requires the navigation data to be both extremely accurate and extremely reliable. This paper describes a method for autonomous GPS satellite failure detection and isolation.

Record 2 of 3
Author(s): Brown, R. G.
Title: A Baseline GPS RAIM Scheme and a Note on the Equivalence of Three RAIM Methods
Source: NAVIGATION, 1992, 39(3), pp.301-316
Year: 1992
DOI: 10.1002/j.2161-4296.1992.tb02272.x
Abstract: The RTCA GPS Minimum Operational Performance Standards have now been released. A considerable portion of the MOPS is devoted to GPS integrity requirements. This paper proposes a baseline RAIM scheme that can serve as a standard for performance comparisons.
